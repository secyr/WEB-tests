Alex Haley

The man who sparked a million questions

A list of his works

  • The Autobiography of Malcolm X (1965)
  • Super Fly T.N.T. (1973), screenplay
  • Roots: The Saga of an American Family (1976), novel
  • Alex Haley Tells the Story of His Search for Roots (1977) ‐ 2-LP recording of a two-hour lecture
  • A Different Kind of Christmas (1988), stories
  • Alex Haley's Queen: The Story of an American Family (1992), novel
  • Alex Haley: The Playboy Interviews (1993), collection
  • Never Turn Back: Father Serra's Mission (Stories of America) (1993), editor, stories
  • Mama Flora's Family (1998), novel

Awards and Recognition

  • 1977: Spingarn Medal from NAACP for his work Roots
  • 1977: Golden Plate Award of the American Academy of Acheievement
  • 1999: USCGC Alex Haley is named after him by the Coast Guard
  • 2002: Korean War Service medal award post-humously by South Korea (Republic of Korea)
  • The food-service building at USCG Training Center, Petaluma is named in his honor
  • The Chief Journalist Alex Haley Award is awarded annually, and is named in his honor as the Coast Guard's first chief journalist.
